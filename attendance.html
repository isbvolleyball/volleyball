Attendance list

  • Signing up, what you need to know

    Do you wish to have fun, learn how to play volleyball and play for free a few hours per week then sign up below.

    If you plan on joining make sure to fill in your name. Once filled in you will be redirected to the relevant attendance sheet.

    There are a limited amount of spots per volleyball session. 28 on mondays and 42 on wednesdays. If the lists are full you will be put on the waiting list so make sure you sign up quickly because it is first come first serve!

    If plans change and you will not be able to make it anymore then please take your name off of the attendance list. Make sure to take your name off of the attendance list as soon as possible so other people have time to check wether they can still show up to volleyball or not.

    Lastly if the list is completely full and there are more people there than there are on the list the people that are not on the list will not be allowed to play.

  • Monday

    On mondays we have 4 courts because we share the gym with the students that are playing basketball.

  • wednesday

    On wednesdays we have the gym all to ourselves which means 6 courts. If there are less than 24 people signed up around 14:00 on the same wednesday then the coordinator will contact basketball and tell them they can come play at the gym tonight.

  • Taking your name off the list

    If you plan on not coming to volleyball anymore then please take your name off of the list. If the list is full it would be greatly appreciated to do this as soon as possible to make room for others who are in the waiting list.
